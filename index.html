Common Questions from Our Clients

Find quick answers to common questions about our services.

A1: RIPS is the Rajasthan Investment Promotion Scheme. It offers government subsidies and tax benefits to new industries, and we help you apply for them.

A2: CTE (Consent to Establish) is needed before construction, while CTO (Consent to Operate) is needed before starting production. We handle both the initial approvals and renewals.

A3: For a new factory, you'll need our help with land arrangement, licensing, industrial approvals (like Fire NOC), pollution consents, and tax setup. We offer a complete package.

A4: We specialize in the manufacturing and industrial sectors. Our clients are often in automotive, electronics, textiles, footwear, and other MSMEs requiring factory setup and compliance services.

A5: Yes. While we are based in Bhiwadi, our services extend to clients in major industrial areas like Kushkhera, Neemrana, Jalore, and anywhere else in Rajasthan.

A6: We provide complete guidance on government incentives. We help you apply for schemes like RIPS, and we actively track your claims to ensure you receive the benefits.
